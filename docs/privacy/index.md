# Revoxa Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-08

## Overview

Revoxa is a local subscription tracking app for iPhone, iPad, and Mac. It helps you record recurring subscriptions, renewal dates, billing cycles, categories, notes, reminders, and estimated costs.

Revoxa is designed to keep your subscription records on your device. The app does not require an account and does not operate a backend service for your personal subscription data.

## Data You Enter

You may enter subscription information such as:

- Subscription name
- Amount and currency
- Billing cycle
- Next billing date
- Category and payment method
- Status
- Reminder settings
- Cancellation URL
- Notes

This information is stored locally on your device using Apple platform storage. It is not sent to Revoxa servers.

## Data Collection

Revoxa does not collect personal data from the app.

Revoxa does not use:

- Third-party advertising SDKs
- Third-party analytics SDKs
- Tracking technologies
- Account registration
- Bank account connections
- Payment card connections
- Email scanning
- Contact, camera, microphone, photo library, or location access

## Exchange Rate Data

Revoxa may fetch public exchange-rate data from the Central Bank of the Republic of Türkiye (TCMB) at:

`https://www.tcmb.gov.tr/kurlar/today.xml`

This request is used to help display converted currency totals. Your subscription records are not included in this request.

## Notifications

If you enable local renewal reminders, Revoxa uses Apple local notification APIs to schedule reminders on your device. Notification scheduling is local to your device.

You can manage notification permission in the system Settings app.

## CSV Export

Revoxa lets you export your subscription data as CSV. Exported files are created only when you choose to export them. You are responsible for where you save and share exported files.

## Data Deletion

You can delete individual subscription records in the app.

You can also clear all local subscription data from Settings. This removes locally stored subscription records from the app.

## Children

Revoxa is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as Revoxa changes. The updated date at the top of this page will be revised when changes are made.

## Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact:

`support@revoxa.app`
