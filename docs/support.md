## Revoxa Support

Revoxa helps you track recurring subscriptions, renewal dates, estimated costs, local reminders, and CSV exports on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

## Common Questions

### Do I need an account?

No. Revoxa does not require an account.

### Where is my data stored?

Your subscription records are stored locally on your device.

### Does Revoxa connect to my bank, card, email, or App Store subscriptions?

No. Revoxa does not connect to banks, payment cards, email inboxes, or your App Store subscription account. You add and manage records manually.

### How do reminders work?

Revoxa can schedule local renewal reminders if you enable notifications. You can manage notification permission in the system Settings app.

### How do I export my data?

Open Settings and use the CSV export option. The exported CSV file is created only when you choose to export it.

### How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual subscriptions from their edit screen. You can also clear all local data from Settings.

### Why do currency totals mention exchange rates?

Revoxa may fetch public exchange-rate data from TCMB to display converted totals. Your subscription records are not sent with that request.

## Troubleshooting

### The app icon does not update in Simulator or macOS

This can happen because of system or simulator icon cache. Delete the old app from the simulator or reinstall the app. On macOS, quit Revoxa and reinstall from the latest build.

### Notifications do not appear

Check system notification permissions for Revoxa, then turn renewal reminders off and on again in the app.

### CSV export is missing

Check the location you selected during export. Revoxa creates CSV files only when you manually start export.
